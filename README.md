# Discord TCG Bot

A community-driven trading card game (TCG) Discord bot with coins, card packs, and mini-games,

---

## Features

- **Currency System**
 - Earn coins with '!earn' and '!daily'
 - Track balances with '!balance'

- **Trading Card Game**
 - Inventory tracking of cards with '!inventory'
 - Collect cards of various rarities: common, rare, epic, legendary
 - Open packs with '!openpack' to receive random cards
 
- **Mini-Games**
 - Slot machine-style gambling with weighted odds
 - Admin commands like '!givecoins'

- **Leaderboard & Admin Commands**
 - Leaderboard to show top coin holders with '!leaderboard'
 - Admins commands like '!givecoins' to manage currency
 
---

## Future Features

- **Multiple Card Packs**
 - Allow users to draw more than one card at a time for more variety and chance to grab rarer cards
 - Higher tiered card packs will cost more coins but provide higher odds of legendaries
 
- **Trading Outpost & Marketplace**
 - Allow users to exchange their cards for rarer or more common cards with other users for more interaction
 - Enable users to trade their coins for card packs by rarity at the Marketplace
 
- **Server Customization**
 - Enable servers to create a unique TCG experience that matches their community theme and style
 - Custom emojis and badges for cards, slots, and leaderboards
 - Configurable coin economy (eg., daily rewards, pack costs)
 
- **Future-Proofing**
 - Server-specific database configuration
 - Logging and analytics for admins
